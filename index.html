Meet The Team

Applied Media Promotions (AMP) is a student-staffed strategic communications firm operated by the Fine and Applied Arts Department at Parkland College. We provide students with the opportunity to enhance their writing, critical thinking, and creative skills by producing promotional materials for organizations at Parkland and non-profits in the Champaign-Urbana community.

Interested in hiring AMP to work for you?

Click here to submit a Project Proposal Form.

We are happy to accept proposals from students, faculty, and staff associated with Parkland College and any not-for-profit organization in the Champaign-Urbana area. We are unable to work with for- profit businesses at this time.

Preference is given to proposals that encourage students to think strategically. Projects that require students to develop comprehensive promotional plans within 16 week semesters are ideal.
