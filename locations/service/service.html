Our Services

AMP provides clients with strategic solutions to promotional challenges related to:

  • Branding and Rebranding
  • Event Planning and Promotion
  • Advertisement Creation
  • Media Relations
  • Website Design and Development
AMP provides these services free-of-charge.

Our Process

  1. Prospective clients fill out a project proposal form.
  2. If the client and AMP team decide to work together, they both sign a contract.
  3. The client fills out a self-evaluation that highlights the strengths and weaknesses of their nonprofit’s current communication strategy.
  4. Armed with this information and notes from the first client meeting, AMP students complete their own research, followed by brainstorming and sketching ideas.
  5. A few weeks into the semester the AMP project team presents a strategic plan and first round of design ideas to the client, who approves the work, or (more likely) asks for changes.
  6. The rest of the semester is spent refining materials with the goal of presenting finished, useable pieces to the client by the time finals begin.

Why use a student-staffed firm?

We believe organizations that utilize our student talent have an advantage for these reasons:

  • Students today are technology natives. They naturally follow emerging technology and understand the latest social media trends.
  • Our students are supervised by faculty members that are experts in their fields—public relations, marketing, graphic design, and web development.
  • If you are trying to reach young people as potential clients, volunteers or donors, students are the perfect consultants to help you target this demographic.
